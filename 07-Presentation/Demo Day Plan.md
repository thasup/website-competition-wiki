# Demo Day Plan

**When:** Thu 16 Jul 10:00–12:00. **Worth:** 15% (Presentation & Knowledge Sharing) + influences Business Story judgment.

## What judges want to hear
1. **The business story** — why Terra is different ("Strategy that ships") and how the site proves it.
2. **How we used Claude** — concrete, reusable techniques other teams can steal. This is the explicit ask ("แชร์ให้ทีมอื่นเอาไปใช้ต่อได้").

## Suggested 12-minute structure
| Time | Segment | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:30 | Hook | The problem: ~70% of transformations stall in the strategy→delivery gap |
| 0:30–2:30 | Story | "Strategy that ships" — [[Differentiation & Positioning Brief]]; show first scroll live |
| 2:30–6:00 | Live walk-through | Pillars → portfolio (Strategy→Delivery cards) → proof → CTA; mobile view; the orb/interaction |
| 6:00–10:00 | **Claude techniques** | Top 5 from [[Claude Utilization Log#"What worked best" (for the demo)]] — one before/after each, spanning Code, Planning, MCP, Skills, Subagents, Image-gen |
| 10:00–11:30 | Takeaways | 3 prompts/techniques other teams can reuse ([[Prompt Library]]) |
| 11:30–12:00 | Close + Q&A | Recap the differentiator; invite hearts (popular vote) |

## Roles
- **Non-dev** drives the story + Claude-technique narrative.
- **Dev** drives the live walk-through + technical depth on Q&A.
