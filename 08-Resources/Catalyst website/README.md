# Catalyst Behavioral Website — Downloaded Assets

Local mirror of every image and video asset served from [catalystbehavioral.com](https://catalystbehavioral.com/), downloaded 2026-07-09 for offline reference while building the teardown.

- `img/` — logos, portraits, team headshots, service icons (svg/png/webp)
- `videos/` — homepage hero background loops (`home-desktop.mp4`, `home-mobile.mp4`)
- `favicon.svg`, `meta.jpg` — site favicon and social share image

Crawled from all 13 site pages (home, team, contact, David Neal bio, and the 9 survey-type landing pages) plus `dist/index.css`.
