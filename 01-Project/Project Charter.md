# Project Charter — TDV Website Hackathon 2026

## Objective
Design and build a **new Terra Digital Ventures marketing website** in 10 days, using **Claude across every phase**, and win on two fronts: a sharp **business story** and demonstrable **Claude mastery**. Reference site: [terradigitalventures.com](https://www.terradigitalventures.com).

## Definition of success (in priority order)
1. **Pass all 8 mandatory requirements** — see [[Mandatory Requirements Tracker]]. Miss one and the entry is incomplete regardless of polish.
2. **Land the differentiator on first scroll** — [[Differentiation & Positioning Brief|"Strategy that ships."]] Business Story is 25% of the score.
3. **Document Claude usage as we go** — [[Claude Utilization Log]]. Claude Utilization is the other 25%.
4. **Ship a deployed, mobile-responsive, interactive site** before Wed 15 Jul 23:59 ICT.
5. **Nail the 12-min demo** — [[Demo Day Plan]]. Presentation + knowledge-sharing is 15%.

## Scope
**In scope:** a multi-section marketing site (Home + supporting pages) covering the 3 pillars, a portfolio, social proof, team, and a clear CTA — built on the [[Design System Overview|TDV design system]].

**Out of scope (unless time allows):** back-end/auth, CMS, real form submission handling, live analytics, i18n beyond the bilingual EN/TH the design system already supports.

## Constraints
- **Hard deadline:** Wed 15 Jul 23:59 ICT (deployed link submitted). See [[Timeline & Milestones]].
- **Team composition rule:** ≥2 people, ≥1 dev **and** ≥1 non-dev. See [[Team & Roles]].
- **Tooling:** Claude must be the primary tool across plan → build → content → present.
- **Assets:** organizers provide only the [[Design System Overview|brand kit]]; **we source/generate all imagery ourselves** (Claude image-gen or Unsplash/Pexels).
