# Competition Brief — TDV Website Hackathon 2026

*Digested from the official [rules page](https://terra-web-hackathon.vercel.app/rules) and [home page](https://terra-web-hackathon.vercel.app/). Source of truth is the site; this is our working copy.*

## The premise
Redesign/rebuild the **Terra Digital Ventures website** in 10 days, using **Claude as the primary tool** end-to-end. The real contest is *using Claude to build*, not just building.

## Eligibility & team rules
- Open to all employees, all functions (interns included).
- Register as a **team of ≥2**.
- Must include **≥1 Dev** and **≥1 Non-Dev** (Advisory / PO / Data / QA / Design / BA / DevOps / Support). Cross-functional by design → [[Team & Roles]].
- No prior Claude experience required.

## Mandatory requirements (all must be present)
Tracked with owners & status in [[Mandatory Requirements Tracker]]. Summary:
1. Terra's differentiation clear on **first scroll** (both Strategic Consulting **and** Technology Delivery).
2. All **3 pillars**: Strategy, Technology, Data Analytics.
3. **Portfolio page** with Industry + Outcome per project.
4. **Social proof** (testimonials or client logos).
5. Clear **CTA**.
6. Shows **people diversity** (Consultant + Technology Specialist).
7. **Mobile responsive.**
8. **≥1 interactive / animation** element.

## The Claude rule
Claude must be primary across at least: **(01) plan & design · (02) code & UI · (03) content & assets · (04) presentation prep.** No proof artifacts required — just narrate techniques at the demo so other teams can reuse them. Capture everything in [[Claude Utilization Log]] and [[MCP, Skills & Subagents]].

## Submission mechanics
- Submit a **deployed link** (e.g. Vercel) via the [form](https://terra-web-hackathon.vercel.app/submit) by the deadline.
- Only the link is required; name/technique/slides optional but recommended.
- Entries shown **anonymously** ("ผลงาน 01, 02…"); can edit until deadline.
- All entries appear in the public gallery → drives the **♥ popular vote**. Mechanics → [[Deployment & Submission]].
